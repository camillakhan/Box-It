John Smith

41, Brooklyn, New York

"As a father of three, full time employee, and little league coach on the side, it can be hard to find time in my schedule for anything. When my kids start running low on school supplies, I continuously put it off until my kids are left with almost nothing to use. Next I have to load up my car for our venture to the nearest supply store, a headache just getting my kids into the car let alone getting them to sit still. Finally, entering the store I'm bombarded with bright sale signs, contradicting prices, and finding exactly what my kids need. Who could forget that monstrous line beginning at register one and mazing around the store seemingly with no end. When register one closed as I was approaching the front of the line I knew there had to be a better way."

Hillary Diane Rodham Clinton (born October 26, 1947) is an American politician. She was United States Secretary of State in the administration of President Barack Obama from 2009 to 2013, a United States Senator representing New York from 2001 to 2009, and, as the wife of President Bill Clinton, First Lady of the United States from 1993 to 2001. A leading candidate for the Democratic Party's nomination to the 2008 presidential election, she has announced her candidacy for the Democratic nomination in the 2016 presidential election.
